What Cloud Connect includes

Cloud Connect is positioned as a customer service for secure remote access and premium cloud capabilities.

Cloud connectivity from anywhere

Move Apex Connect devices and dashboards from local-only access to secure remote availability through your Cloud account.

Security and privacy

Each account is activated through billing or service approval, with remote access tied to customer identity and account state.

Simple annual pricing

Annual pricing is shown during secure checkout. Your cloud address is reserved during signup and service starts once the account is enabled.

Voice assistant connectivity

Easily connect supported devices and routines to voice assistants through your Cloud account for hands-free control.

Cloud text-to-speech

The Cloud text-to-speech (TTS) service allows you to have text read aloud with natural sounding voices. This service is automatically enabled when you are signed in to your ApexOS Cloud account.

Automation webhooks

Use webhooks to trigger automations and notify external systems without exposing your local network directly to the internet.

Improved camera streaming

Get improved camera streaming with WebRTC for faster live views and a better remote monitoring experience.

One cloud account

Account access, billing state, service approval and your reserved cloud address stay together in one customer account.
